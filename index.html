school church

This schools church still remains a memory for the entire school. People used to always study their prayers every single day before entering the school.

school library

Was built in 1980

This schools library was a good place for the students to study. A lot of students used to hate studying, so they often started to escape school, or fake something like being sick, or making ecscuses. So that is why the school built this library in 1980.

school auditorium

Was built in 1982

This schools auditorium was such a memorable place. The students always loved to see all sorts of performances in the school auditorium. There used to be concerts, magicians coming on the stage and doing unbelievable magic tricks, and a lot more.
